Paneer Butter Masala

  • Paneer - 250g
  • Butter - 2 tbsp
  • Tomato puree - 1 cup
  • Spices - Garam masala, chili powder, etc.
  1. Heat butter in a pan.
  2. Add tomato puree and cook.
  3. Add spices and paneer cubes.
  4. Simmer until thick and creamy.

Chicken Curry

  • Chicken - 500g
  • Onions - 2 (sliced)
  • Tomatoes - 2 (chopped)
  • Spices - Turmeric, chili powder, garam masala
  1. Fry onions until golden.
  2. Add tomatoes and spices, cook till soft.
  3. Add chicken and cook until done.
  4. Simmer with water until gravy thickens.
